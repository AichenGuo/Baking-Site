Banana Bread

Ingredients

For 6-inch cake mold or equivalent
Banana 2-3
Egg 2
Butter 70g
Cake flour 180g
Sugar 50g
Baking soda 5g
Walnuts & Chia Seeds adjustable

Steps

  1. Warm bananas in the microwave for 1-1.5 minutes to boost its flavor.
  2. Remove the Banana Skin. Wear a food-safe glove and use hand to squish the bananas into mashes.
  3. Add eggs, butter, and sugar into the container of banana mashes. Mix them.
  4. Sieve cake flour and baking sada into the last container. Use baking spatula to mix them together in a "Z" way.
  5. Add your preferred amount of walnuts and chia seeds (usually about 50~60g) into the mix. You may substitute them to other nuts/dry fruits you like.
  6. Preheat the oven to 340℉.
  7. Put a sheet of oilpaper inside the baking mold. Pour the mix into the baking mold. You can scatter some more nuts on the top of the mix and gently press them down.
  8. Put the mold on the middle layer of the oven to bake for about 40 minutes.
  9. Once the baking process is done, take the mold out from the oven and lie the bread on its side to cool down.
  10. Enjoy!
