Pork Sung Cream Lava Cake

Ingredients

6-inch Chiffon Cake
Yolk Mix
Yolk 3
Sugar 13g
Corn oil 30g
Milk 40g
Cake flour 60g
Egg White Mix
Egg White 3
Sugar 35g
Lemonade A few drops
Cream Lava & Pork Sung
Heavy whipping cream 150g
Sugar 15g
Milk 18g
Salt 1g
Pork Sung adjustable

Steps

  1. Separate yolk and egg whites into two different containers. Keep egg whites chill for later use.
  2. Pour the prepared milk, sugar, and corn oil into a clean container. Use hand whisk to mix them well. You can put in a few drops of vanillin if you wish.
  3. Sieve cake flour into the last container. Use baking spatula to mix them together in a "Z" way.
  4. Add yolk into the mix, and use hand whisk to mix them well in a "Z" way.
  5. Use spatula to make sure the yolk mix does not contain any flour chunks.
  6. Take out the egg whites, and add in a few drops of lemonade. Add in sugar in three times while using electric whisk to beat up egg whites until you can pull out an upright egg white tip.
  7. Pour in 1/3 egg whites mix into the yolk mix, and use spatula to mix them well in irrugular directions.
  8. Pour the mix back to the rest of egg whites mix and use spatula to mix them well.
  9. Preheat the oven to 305 ℉. Without putting in oil papers inside the cake mold, pour the mix into the mold from a height of 15cm. Gently shake the mold to remove bubbles.
  10. Once preheat is completed, put the cake mold into the middle lower layer of the oven to bake for 55 minutes.
    • During the baking process, the batter will grow taller and may burst, which does not matter.
    • You may use a stick to test if the cake is done. If the stick does not take any blatter out, then the cake is well-baked.
  11. Take out the cake mold from the oven once it is done. Drop it from a height of 15cm twice to allow the heat go out. Flip it down to let it cool down.
  12. After two hours of cooling down, use hand to carefully press the edge of the cake to remove it from the mold and take the cake out.
  13. Cut a hole from the bottom of the cake to fill in cream lava later (save the cut out piece).
  14. To make cream lava, put heavy whipping cream, sugar, milk, and a little bit of salt into a bowl, and use electric whisk to beat them until the fluid reaches a yogurt texture.
  15. Pour a little cream lava into the hole of the cake, and put the cut out piece back.
  16. Flip the cake and pour the rest of lava on the cake's surface.
  17. Scatter some pork sung on the top. You can also substitute pork sung with other things you like, e.g. Oreos, fruits, boba, etc.
  18. Enjoy!
