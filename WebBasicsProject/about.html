About Grace Guo

My Childhood

I grew up in Beijing, China. After graduating from UC Berkeley for my undergraduate degree, I now live in San Francisco. With passion in design, I decided to major in Information Experience Design at Pratt Institute for a master degree. And I plan to live in New York for a while once the pandemic is over. Feel free to visit my online portfolio website to know more about me.

My Baking Journey

Similar to many people, I started to experiment on food during the quarantine in 2020. I had my first cake baked in August when my friend had her 22 birthday. And then my baking journey began.
Having tried over 10 baking recipes in the past two months, I decide to find a way to note down the recipes I've used and modified, starting with a few of my favorites. I wish to use this website as a form of documentation for myself, and hopefully, it can also help others in their baking journey.
