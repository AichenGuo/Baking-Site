Basque Cheesecake

Ingredients

For 6-inch cake mold
Cream Cheese 300g
Whole Egg 150g
Yolk 10g
Heavy whipping cream 170g
Sugar 80g
Corn Starch 4g
Cake flour 8g
Lemonade a few drops

Steps

  1. Put cream cheese and sugar into a container. Use spatula to mix them together.
  2. Pour in egg liquid that was mixed well. Use electric whisk to mix well.
  3. Add in heavy whipping cream and a few drops of lemonade. Use electric whisk to mix them.
  4. Sieve cake flour and corn starch into the container. Use hand whisk to mix them together.
  5. Preheat the oven to 430℉.
  6. Put a sheet of oilpaper inside the baking mold and pour the mix into the baking mold.
  7. Put the mold on the middle layer of the oven to bake for about 20~23 minutes until the surface has a burnt sugar coloring.
  8. Once the baking process is done, take the mold out from the oven and let the cheesecake cool down.
  9. After cooling down, put the cheesecake inside the refrigerator for 5 hours to maximize its taste. Enjoy!
